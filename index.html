About

I am an ML scientist and a Biostatistics & Bioinformatics PhD candidate at Emory University. I build statistical and machine-learning methods for high-dimensional biomedical data — across genomics, single-cell data, and medical imaging.

Previously, I was a research scientist in medical AI, where I built production deep-learning pipelines for brain MRI, supported FDA-cleared products, and co-invented several granted U.S. patents. I'm now seeking ML Scientist / Research Scientist roles where strong statistics meets applied machine learning.
